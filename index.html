About Us

AKCSSC '21 is the sixth edition of All Kerala Computer Society Student Convention conducted by IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter and co-hosted by Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology. The event spans 3-days from 26th to 28th November 2021. Going with the theme 'Digital Transformation and Entrepreneurship', there are numerous online and offline events to be conducted. The event comprises workshops, tech talk sessions, competitions, chapter presentations, virtual IV, networking sessions and cultural programs.

  • AKCSSC provides a platform for all participants to foster technical prowess.
  • It is an opportunity to meet and network with like-minded students and professionals which helps to gain experience and develop skills.
  • The event is a blend of technical and cultural activities, thus exploring the limitless technology with a lot of fun.

To promote entrepreneurship among students, the Entrepreneurship and Innovation wing of IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter is hosting 'SOT: Startups of Tomorrow' as a part of AKCSSC 2021 in collaboration with ADIF, Kerala Startup Mission and IEEE PIE Kerala Section. SOT will be held between November 26 - 28, 2021. This event will boost collaboration among students and startups. The main aim of the expo is to bring together student entrepreneurs from various backgrounds and provide an opportunity for them to interact and network with like-minded people.

  • SOT will consist of five events, which are product expo, idea pitching competition, panel discussion, a talk session and a competition.
  • These competitions and events will surely be an unforgettable experience and help you grow your career.

Events

Events under SOT: Startups of Tomorrow

StartUps Innovation Expo

An entirely virtual event where various startups showcase their premium products and services. The event runs from November 26 - 28, 2021 with free registration. The event is open to all public but the participating startups should meet the eligibility criteria of Startup India. Cash prize INR 20,000.

Pitch Perfect

An idea pitching competition will be held during AKCSSC 2021 exclusively for students. The event aims to promote brainstorming and sharing of ideas to improve them and make them a reality. The competition will comprise of two categories - IT and General. Cash prize INR 10,000 each.

Panel discussion

An intriguing panel discussion will be conducted on the topic “How can startups compete in the monopolistic market”. The event will be held virtually on 27th November 2021, at 6:00pm (IST). The panelists will be aspiring as well as renowned personalities from different areas of the topic.

ANALIZO

ANALIZO is the 9th task, exclusively for IEEE members and will consist of two rounds. Students must participate in teams of three - CEO, COO and CTO. During the initial round, multiple topics will be given to the participants such as machine learning, deep learning, software development and so on and each team can choose a company from the given fields.

Talk Session

A talk session on the topic "Entrepreneurship in Cybersecurity" will be conducted as a part of SOT held during AKCSSC 2021 by IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter. The event will be on 27th November at 09:00AM (IST). (speaker details)

Frequently Asked Questions

How to participate in the events of the SOT?

You have to register as a delegate of AKCSSC 2021 to participate in the events of the Expo.

Can I vote for the product Expo?

Active IEEE CS Kerala Chapter members as well as AKCSSC delegates can vote in the product Expo.

How can I vote for the products in the product Expo?

You have to click the login button in the Expo page, then enter your IEEE number and Email ID you have registered with IEEE and click Login. Once you are successfully logged in, you can vote for the products.

How many products can I vote for?

You can vote for only 1 product.

How many participants are required for the pitching competition?

Individual participation or a team of 2 or 3 members is allowed.
